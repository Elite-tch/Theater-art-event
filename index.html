About the Event

That's A Wrap Film Fest is a dynamic platform dedicated to empowering the next generation of Nollywood filmmakers. Our mission is to spotlight emerging talent, providing young filmmakers with the opportunity to showcase their work, learn from industry veterans, and connect with key players in the Nigerian film industry. Whether you're creating short films, documentaries, or groundbreaking features, TAWFF is your launchpad into Nollywood’s future.
